\\(f(x)\\): \\(f\\) of \\(x\\).

\\(k*f(x)\\): \\(k\\) times a function \\(f\\) where \\(k\\) is a factor and \\(f(x)\\) is a factor.

\\(k\*x + k\*y = k\*(x+y)\\): Factorization. Decomposition into a product of multiple factors.
